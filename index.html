Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat heavy cream and butter over medium heat until butter is melted.
  3. Stir in Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until cheese is melted and sauce is smooth.
  4. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and stir until heated through.
  5. Combine sauce with cooked fettuccine and toss until pasta is coated.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
